Who I Am

From engineering to product thinking.

I started as an Electronics and Communication Engineering undergraduate at VIT Vellore — curious about how systems work, not just how they're built.

That curiosity led me through cybersecurity, then into cloud infrastructure, before I found what stuck: agentic AI. Each step added a layer to how I think about building things that hold up under real use.

Today I work on real manufacturing and operational problems — turning scattered reports and reactive workflows into AI products a team can actually rely on, while contributing to and managing technical events with a combined reach of 800+ participants at E-Cell VIT.
